James on defeat: What changes should the team make? A good many

Beijing time on November 28, the NBA regular season continued, the Lakers lost 94-138 away from the 76ers. After the game, Lakers player lebron James gave an interview. When asked what changes the Lakers should make in a 44-point blowout, James replied, "What changes? A lot." The reporter asked, Is there anything that needs to be changed? Again, James replied, "A lot."
